Pet E-Profile

A pet e-profile is a centralized digital record containing essential pet information: breed details, genetic history, vaccinations, and treatment records. In a connected system, this e- profile is accessible to relevant platforms and clinics, streamlining care and improving identification accuracy.

AI Veterinary Prognosis

Owners can upload pet images or describe their pet's conditions to receive AI- generated medical insights and care recommendations.

Veterinary Consultations

The platform integrates certified NZ vets for image, voice, and video consultations. Consultation data syncs with pets' digital records, ensuring care continuity.
